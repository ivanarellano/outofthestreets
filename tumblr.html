Running 13.1 miles isn't easy, which is why I've signed up for a half-marathon on April 14, 2013. Through training and motivation to get in shape, I'll be hitting the track once more to cross the finish line. But I can't compete alone.

Your donation, no matter the size, will benefit my cause and go further than my mileage this event.

Help students succeed

All proceeds will benefit City Year, a national nonprofit which is working to keep students in-school and on-track for a successful high school graduation.

Working side-by-side with teachers and students, corps members will engage students in tutoring with reading and math, and by providing after school programming.
